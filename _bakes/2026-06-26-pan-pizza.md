## Levain
 - Checked at 10hrs and hadn't risen much because it was in cool area (72F?) so moved to warmer area near laundry fridge
 - Let rise for 14.5hrs total because it wasn't super bubbly and I had the time

## Mix
 - 6F friction factor
 - Mixed for around 7 minutes before putting in the oil because it wasn’t strengthing. (Still wasn’t very strong after 7 minutes.) Mixed for 3.5min after adding oil and it seemed to be a bit more strengthened and basically clung around dough hook.
 - Final dough temp: 84.5F–probably because I mixed it for so long, use 11f friction factor next time

## Bulk Fermentation & Proof
 - Rose really well actually (around 2x) which was surprising because it was not much dough in a large bowl

## Shape
 - Just put a light layer of flour on the sheet pan and put the dough directly on it, which worked pretty well (not that much stickage)
 - Pulled too much of the dough to the edge so it was thick on the outside and thin on the inside → <u>make sure to not bring too much dough to the edge next time</u>

## Bake
 - Did both at the same time at upper and lower thirds of the oven and switched+rotated halfway through, but the tops got browned way quicker than the bottom so the bottoms never got much color → <u>maybe extend the pre-toppings cook or cook them separately (at different times or in different ovens) next time</u>
