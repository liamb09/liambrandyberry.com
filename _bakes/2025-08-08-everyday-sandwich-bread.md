## Levain
 - Had risen well by the morning

## Mix
 - Final dough temperature: 77F
 - Use 5F friction flour for bread dough.
 - Don’t be afraid to extend slap and fold method until dough looks good and feels strong, up to double, especially because I’m kinda slow and inefficient at it.

## Shape
 - Dough just stuck to parchment sling, creating wrinkles in the dough so that it wasn’t flush with the sides of the pan. It was more successful without a sling; just grab the dough by the ends with a hand and a bench scraper, not from the middle.

## Proof
 - If the proof seems to be on schedule, preheat oven at least 20min beforehand so it’s ready.

## Bake
 - Despite forgetting to turn the temperature down, they didn’t burn and the internal temperature was only a couple degrees higher. Maybe it had to do with our bad oven.
