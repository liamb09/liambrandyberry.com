## Levain
 - Active

## Mixing
 - Extra sticky

## Final Dough Temperature: 80F

## Warm Bulk Fermentation
 - Left on counter (75-76F?) so it didn't get too hot, probably cooled a bit then

## Shaping
 - Used flour generously, really sealed baguettes with palm of hand; overall went really well

## Pre-Baking
 - Transferring to parchment for scoring before baking was fine; some stuck to towel a bit &rarr; <u>add more flour next time</u>
 - Baguettes were close together so slightly flattened neighboring ones when getting them out &rarr; <u>give them more space</u>


## Baking
 - Had to take both batches out 5ish minutes early because they were getting really dark, but I don't think the temperature really needs to be lowered


## Result
 - Fantastic crust (crunchy but not hard) and crumb (open); really good flavor
 - Staled by the day after but toasting revived it
