## Levain
 - Had risen well and was very bubbly by the morning.

## Mix
 - Extend mixing time if necessary until it resists your pull; I did a little more than double here.
 - Use a 14.5F friction factor for pizza dough in the stand mixer next time.

## Warm Bulk Fermentation
 - Had barely risen, if at all, when I moved it into the fridge.

## Cold Bulk Fermentation
 - Hadn’t risen significantly by the end, but had a small bubble or two and domed very slightly down at the edges of the bowl.

## Divide and Preshape
 - Dough was surprisingly not sticky, likely because it was cold and a little wet from condensation.

## Proof
 - Dough balls flattened/spread significantly.
 - Oil on dough balls definitely helped prevent sticking, so do that next time.
